cryptography

Cryptography relies on math basics like prime numbers and algebra for secure data encryption. Concepts such as modular arithmetic and finite fields are crucial in developing encryption methods like RSA. Probability theory helps assess cryptographic strength and analyze attack likelihood. These mathematical foundations ensure data confidentiality, integrity, and authenticity against adversaries. Understanding these principles is essential for effective cybersecurity strategies.

Turkish course

The Turkish language course for A1 certification is designed for beginners aiming to develop foundational skills in Turkish communication. Through interactive lessons, learners will grasp essential vocabulary, grammar rules, and conversational phrases necessary for basic communication in everyday situations. Topics covered include introductions, greetings, expressing needs and preferences, and simple conversations about family, work, and daily routines. Emphasis is placed on practical language use, with listening, speaking, reading, and writing exercises tailored to facilitate comprehension and fluency at the beginner level. By the end of the course, students will be prepared to navigate basic interactions in Turkish-speaking environments and to undertake the A1 Turkish certification exam with confidence
